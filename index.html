The mission

Star Wars through a different lens.

Correlius uses myth and science fiction to explore Black emotional life, neurodivergence, family, faith, trauma, and recovery.

This is not a story about becoming the strongest person in the room. It is about learning to remain yourself in a world that keeps asking you to become someone else.
